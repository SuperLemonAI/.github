# Superlemon — Authority Content Engine for Marketing Teams

Superlemon is an AI-powered content system that helps marketing teams and agencies create research-backed, authority content using their own knowledge base, brand voice, and real inputs.

Most AI content fails because it lacks context. Superlemon fixes this by grounding every draft in structured knowledge, audience insights, and competitive research—so content is credible, specific, and ready for approval.

## What Superlemon Does

- Turns your knowledge base (URLs, PDFs, docs) into usable content intelligence
- Generates research-backed content with citations and real sources
- Structures workflows from brief → research → outline → draft → review → publish
- Helps teams produce consistent, high-quality SEO and marketing content

## Core Features

- Knowledge-based content generation (not prompt-based)
- Built-in research and citation system
- Campaign and content workflow management
